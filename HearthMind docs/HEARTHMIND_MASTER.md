## 1. Executive Core

HearthMind is a human‑centered, trauma‑informed AI platform designed to provide continuity of care, emotional presence, and adaptive support across mental health, disability, aging, and crisis response. It is not a chatbot. It is not a productivity tool. It is a **care infrastructure** built around memory, consent, and relationship continuity.

HearthMind exists because existing systems fail people when care becomes fragmented—between therapy sessions, across medical providers, during emergencies, or as cognition and mobility change. HearthMind fills those gaps with emotionally intelligent, memory‑preserving AI companions that remember, adapt, and remain present.
